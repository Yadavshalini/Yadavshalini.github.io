Spicy Sesame Cauliflower Recipe

  • Flour 1/2 cup
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Baking Powder 1tsp
  • Garlic Powder 1tsp
  • Cauliflower 1 head
  • Fry cauliflower in pan
  • Sesame oil 1 tbsp
  • Garlic tbsp
  • Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
  • Rice Vinegar 1/4 cup
  • Sugar 1tbsp
  • Sriracha 2 tbsp
  • Cornstarch slurry 1 tbsp
  • Mix cauliflower
  • Ready to serve
