Chocolate Crazy Cake

  • Flour 1 cup
  • Cocoa Powder3 tbsp
  • Baking soda 1 tsp
  • Granulated Sugar 1 cup
  • Kosher salt 1/2 tsp
  • Create 3 wells
  • White Vinegar 1 tsp
  • Pure Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
  • Vegetable oil 5 tbsp
  • Water 1 cup
  • Bake at 350 for 35 min
  • Allow to cool down
  • Top it with chocolate or Nutella
